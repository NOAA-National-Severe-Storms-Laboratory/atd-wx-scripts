The two scripts in atd-wx-scripts/alford-grl-qlcs are assocaited with the article to Geophysical Research Letters entitled "Dual-Polarization Phased Array Radar Observations of Quasi-Linear Convective System Tornadic Mesovortices."

Citation: Alford, A. A., C. M. Kuster, and R. L. Miller, 2025: Dual-Polarization Phased Array Radar Observations of Quasi-Linear Convective System Tornadic Mesovortices. Geophysical Research Letters, in review.

The ATD data relevant to the included scripts are housed at data.nssl.noaa.gov.

The SAMURAI analyses relevant to the included scripts are housed at DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.15485125.
